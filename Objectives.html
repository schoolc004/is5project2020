Objectives of the Study

General Objective

This study aims to design and develop a Parking Area Availability System Specific Objectives

1. This study aimed to:

a) manage user;

b) manage device;

c) determine availability of parking space online/offline;

2. Evaluate the system based on ISO 25010 in terms of functional suitability, performance efficiency, compatibility, usability, reliability, security maintainability and portability.
